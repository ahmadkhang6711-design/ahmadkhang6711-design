General Questions

Is the Ultimate Conversion Toolkit free to use?

Yes, all our conversion tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees or limitations. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone. You can convert as many files as you need without any restrictions.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No account creation is required. Simply upload your files or enter your values, and our tools will handle the conversion instantly. We value your privacy and don't collect personal information unless you choose to provide it.

Can I use the tools on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can access all our conversion tools from any device with an internet connection.

Is there a limit to how many files I can convert?

There are no limits on the number of files you can convert. However, for batch processing, there may be a limit on the number of files you can process at once to ensure optimal performance for all users.

Privacy & Security

How long are my converted files stored?

All files are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours to ensure your privacy and security. We recommend downloading your converted files immediately after conversion. We do not retain any copies of your files.

Are my files secure during conversion?

Yes, we take security seriously. All file transfers are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. Our servers are secured with industry-standard security measures to protect your data during the conversion process.

Do you share my files or personal information with third parties?

No, we do not share your files or personal information with third parties. Your privacy is important to us, and we adhere to strict data protection policies as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Can anyone else access my uploaded files?

No, your uploaded files are only accessible to you during the conversion session. Each conversion session is isolated, and files cannot be accessed by other users or our staff except for maintenance purposes.

Technical Questions

What file formats do you support?

We support a wide range of formats including images (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, SVG), documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, RTF, HTML), videos (MP4, AVI, MOV, WMV, FLV, WebM), and audio files (MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG, M4A). We're constantly adding support for more formats.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes, our batch conversion feature allows you to upload and convert multiple files simultaneously, saving you time and effort. Simply select all the files you want to convert, choose the output format, and start the conversion process.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

The maximum file size varies depending on the type of conversion. For most file types, the limit is 100MB per file. For video conversions, the limit may be lower due to processing requirements. We display the current limits in the tool interface.

Why did my conversion fail?

Conversions can fail for several reasons: corrupted source files, unsupported formats, file size exceeding limits, or temporary server issues. Please ensure your file is not corrupted, check the format compatibility, and try again. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

Can I customize conversion settings?

Yes, many of our tools offer customizable settings. For image conversions, you can adjust quality, resize dimensions, and maintain aspect ratio. For document conversions, you can select specific pages or formatting options. The available settings depend on the type of conversion you're performing.
